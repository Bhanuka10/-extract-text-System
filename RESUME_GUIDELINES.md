### ‚úÖ Recommended Resume Structure

1. **Name at the Top**
   - Place your full name at the very beginning
   - Example: "John Michael Smith"

2. **Contact Information**
   - Include email: yourname@email.com
   - Include phone: +1-555-123-4567 or (555) 123-4567

3. **Clear Section Headers**
   - Use headers like: SKILLS, EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, WORK HISTORY
   - This helps the parser identify sections

4. **Skills Section**
   - List skills explicitly
   - Use keywords from job descriptions
   - Example: "Python, JavaScript, React, Leadership"

5. **Education**
   - Include degree name: Bachelor, Master, MBA, PhD
   - Mention field of study: Computer Science, Business Administration

6. **Experience**
   - Include date ranges: 2019 - 2021 or 2020 - Present
   - Mention years: "5+ years of experience"
